Content & sources

Surf-condition data, spot characteristics and venue information on WaveBase are compiled from public sources (recent reviews on Booking, TripAdvisor, Windguru, Beach-Inspector, OpenStreetMap and others), labelled per entry under each spot's "Source" line. Where personal observation by the editors has been used, this is noted as such. We do our best to keep entries accurate; if you spot an error, please email info@wavebase.com.

Affiliate disclosure

Some "Book now" buttons link to third-party booking platforms. If you book through such a link, WaveBase may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This commission helps keep the site free for surfers. Editorial coverage of every place is independent of any commercial relationship — see our About page for the editorial firewall promise.
