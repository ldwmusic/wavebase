Why are the waves like they are — sand-bottom A-frames?

The river Oued Tamraght has deposited sand here over thousands of years. On that sandy bottom the swell forms sand-bars that shift with storms, so the wave breaks into loose A-frame peaks rather than one long clean wave. Forgiving for beginners; less clean lines for advanced surfers.
